04 — fire it up

par-cooked by us.
finished by you.

Every mira tortilla leaves our kitchen 90% done. The last 40 seconds happen in your pan — that final toast is what makes them taste fresh-made.

  1. dry pan, high heat

    Pan, griddle or grill — no oil. Get it properly hot, about 15–20 seconds per side.

  2. see bubbles? flip it

    When tiny bubbles start forming, flip to the other side.

  3. 15–20 more — it puffs up

    Ready to eat! Stack them in a clean cloth so they stay warm and soft.

keep refrigerated · 7 days  /  or freeze · 3 months
